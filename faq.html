Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question for us? Check to see if it's answered below.

Is there a membership fee?

Nope! No fee. We have some Girls Who Code shirts that are $15, email us if you're interested! Otherwise, the club is completely free.


Do I have to go to every meeting to stay in the club?

Not at all. We don't want you to feel forced to attend every meeting. Throughout the semester we hold a variety of meetings including skills workshops, interview prep, and social events! Feel free to pick and choose which meetings appeal to you. Some members are interested in meeting other girls in their major, and others are interested in further developing their Computer Science skills. We want to provide everyone with the best GWC experience and have different events to target different interests.


Can I join the GWC board?

At the end of every year we will send out applications to see if you are a good fit for one of our positions. We take into account meeting attendance, participation, and your ideas for improving the club in the years to come!


Who is this club for?

If you identify as a girl (non-binary and trans too!) with an interest in computer science, you are welcome to join us. Girls Who Code aims to close the gender gap in technology and change the image of what a programmer looks like.


It's the middle of the semester, can I still join the club?

Of course you can! We'd be happy to have you. As mentioned in a previous answer, we have different meetings throughout the semester, and most of them do not require having attended previous meetings. You're welcome to pick and choose whichever meetings align with your goals and interests, no matter what point in the semester it's in.
