Your Post Must Include One of Our Hashtags...

#INTRO
Tell us who you are, what you're working on + what you'd love to get from the group. Don't be shy--we’re SO excited to meet you!

#YEPIMADETHAT or #YEPIDIDTHAT
You made it, be proud of it! Share your projects + exciting news.

#ASKS
The group works best when we are generous with each other. Feel free to use this group to #ASK about anything you may need help with, but please write a thoughtful ask and tell us a bit about you and why you’re asking.

#RESOURCE
If you have a very useful resource that you think members of the club would find useful, feel free to share it here *with context!* Examples of good resources include: information about an upcoming presentation, available internship positions, etc.

#DISCUSS
You can use #DISCUSS to share an interesting article related to tech. Be specific about what kind of feedback you’d like to hear in the comments.
